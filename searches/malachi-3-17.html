Malachi 3:17
""They will be mine," says the LORD Almighty, "in the day when I make up my treasured possession. I will spare them, just as in compassion a man spares his son who serves him."
Context: Malachi was written in the post-exilic period, around the 5th century BCE, when the Israelites were facing spiritual apathy and social injustice. The message emphasizes God's commitment to His people and the importance of faithfulness amidst their struggles.
Reflection: Today, this verse reminds believers of their identity as God's treasured possession. It encourages individuals to remain faithful and compassionate, knowing that God values and protects them, much like a loving parent cares for their child.
